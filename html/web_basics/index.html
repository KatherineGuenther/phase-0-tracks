This is the first of many websites devoted to Vinnie The Wonder Cat!

Vinnie is a 12 year old Maine Coon who thinks he's a dog. If he knew anything at all about dogs, he'd know that they enjoy long car rides. Vinnie does not...

A few things you should know about Vinnie

  • He doesn't like to discuss his weight.
  • He doesn't talk to strangers.

A few things you should know about Maine Coons

  1. They have giant bodies and tiny voices.
  2. They rarely wear bow ties.
  3. They often think they are predators when they're actually the prey.

Vinnie encourages you to learn more about Maine Coons.
